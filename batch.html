Frequently Asked Questions

📅 Course Duration: 12 months 🎥 Classes: Hybrid Mode (Live & Recorded) 📚 Practice Material Provided

What is the duration of the course?

📅 The course duration is 12 months, with engaging classes throughout the year for full mastery of the syllabus.

Are the classes recorded or live?

🎥 All classes are conducted in hybrid mode, meaning you get both live interactive sessions and full recorded access—so you can learn at your own pace!

What study/practice materials are provided?

📚 We provide comprehensive practice materials, including detailed notes, up-to-date question banks, and previous year papers—all tailored for top competitive success.

How are students evaluated during the course?

📝 We conduct regular class tests to ensure consistent progress and understanding of concepts. In addition, monthly mock tests are organized to simulate real exam conditions, helping students assess their preparedness and adapt their strategies for competitive exams.
